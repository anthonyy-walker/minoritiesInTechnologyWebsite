DSS
Developer Student Seminar (DSS) is an event where students can hear from industry professionals in the computer science and technology fields. This allows students to gain a better knowledge of the real work environment and also serves as a networking opportunity.
Join Our Discord
STC
Student Technology Compitition (STC) is an event hosted by MiT where students get the chance to submit their best work from the following categories: Web/Software development, 3D Animation, and Mechanical. The winner of each category will receive recognition for their work and a cash prize.
Join Our Discord
CFS
Charity For Students (CFS) is a giving event where people can donate to MiT. 100% of the proceeds donated by the publicgoes to our students' college payments.
